FAQ'S

What if I miss my Live class?

Don’t worry! We record every session of the class then store into our video library. You can easily find your missed class session

How will I know that I am a graduate now?

Once you complete all your projects and get good results in your final exam then, the system will notify you that “you are graduated”. Moreover, you will get your certificate in hands within 5-10 days.

How much does the course cost me?

It depends on you, what course you chose for you. Every course has different pricing so you must check out courses pricing and later pay it for your course.

Is there any scholarship available?

Affirmatively! Yes, we do offer a scholarship for the bright one. We give this aid based on performance criteria. If you gave a good performance then, apply for it.

Does this platform give any discount of course?

We don’t have any discount offers yet. Once you enrol yourself on this platform you will receive specific promotional offers from time to time.

What if I don’t receive my scholarship?

If it ever happens with you then you immediately connect with us. We interact with you on a phone call to make sure you receive what you deserve.

Will I charge for my subscription after I graduate?

Once you graduate in your chosen course, you will not be charged for the next month's billing cycle.

When I cancel my subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time without any hassle.
